About Momentum

The Momentum Speaker Series is a TED-like, student led speaker series designed to help Engineering students gain momentum in their career searches. The goal of Momentum is to crowd source advice from peers who have succeeded and/or are experts in certain fields.

Intern Perspective

Hear from peers that have successfully landed internships at top Fortune 500 companies. Topics include how to land a top internship and key take-aways.

Entrepreneurship

Learn from current students who have started their own start-ups or have significant entrepreneurial experience.

Resilience

Talks centered around overcoming failure and other obstacles. Understand that your career path can take dips and turns, not always linearly bound.
